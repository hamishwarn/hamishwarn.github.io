About Me

Hi, I'm Hamish! I am 20 years old and currently pursuing a Bachelor of Mathematical Sciences (Advanced) at Adelaide University. I am an avid enjoyer of everything mathematics related, and over the last year or so I have been working on a large solution compilation of difficult integrals/sums/products. The purpose of this website is primarily to showcase this compilation. Please see 'My Projects' to check it out for yourself!
